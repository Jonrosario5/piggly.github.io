Our Mission

Pigly is a transportation service that provides on-demand piggyback rides to help users get around in the city without using their own feet. Despite major commuting initiatives, the majority of Americans are still driving to work. We are on a mission to cut down the number of cars and vehicles on the road which means less pollution in the air, and further protecting the environment to keep it clean!
