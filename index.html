Our Mission

Pigly is a transportation service that provides on-demand
piggyback rides to help users get around in the city without
using their own feet.

We are on a mission to help you save your energy and replace
your walking time with piggyback rides! In addition to choosing
Pigly as your mode of transportation, you would further cut
down the number of vehicles on the road and continue to
protect our environment.

“What a great way for me to spend less on my
commute and meet new people in my
neighborhood. Plain and simple, piggyback
riding is the future!”
